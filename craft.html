Activia
Rebrand
Elevate Activia from a functional product to a vibrant, sustainable choice in a balanced lifestyle.
Role: Lead Designer
2024
Packaging Design
Commercial Ad
Activia evolves from a clinical gut-health brand into a vibrant, sustainable lifestyle choice that energizes health-conscious, sustainable consumers.
The aim of the rebrand is to move away from sterile packaging and adopt a vibrant visual identity with warm colors like coral, mint, and terracotta. This approach will create an inviting feel, appealing to younger demographics who appreciate joyful and lively products while being mindful of their environmental impact. It will elevate Activia from a functional product to a vibrant, sustainable choice in a balanced lifestyle. By adopting a warm design, emphasizing feel-good messaging, and showcasing commitment to sustainability, Activia will connect with 2024 consumers who seek enjoyment and peace of mind while prioritizing eco-friendly living.

RoomEase
App
Roomease simplifies apartment hunting for students by matching them with compatible roommates and listings.
Role: Lead Designer
2024
UI/UX
Prototyping
RoomEase allows post-secondary students to find housing and roommates in one place, making the leasing process more approachable and less stressful.
Roomease aims to streamline the often complicated and time-consuming process of finding a place to live, focusing specifically on the unique needs and challenges faced by students. By employing intuitive tools, detailed profiles, and intelligent matching algorithms, Roomease not only helps users discover compatible roommates who share their lifestyles and interests, but also guides them toward affordable, comfortable housing options that truly fit their needs. The platform aspires to foster a strong, student-centric community where members feel supported as they settle into new environments, form meaningful connections, and adapt smoothly to the next stage of their academic and personal journeys.

This poster is a clear representation of my core values as a designer: useful, thoughtful, and well-made.
